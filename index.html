FAQ

What's the minimum amount?

You can start with as little as $1,000 depending on the project. Each project has its own minimum investment explicitly stated in its details.
+

How do payouts work?

Payouts from rental yield are distributed quarterly directly to your connected bank account or digital wallet.
+

Can I sell my share if I need to?

Yes. While real estate is a long-term investment, we have a secondary market where you can list your shares to other investors after a 12-month lock-in period.
+

What if something happens to my share?

Your shares are legally backed by a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) that owns the property. You have legal ownership rights equivalent to your share percentage.
+

Is my investment from my bed?

Yes! You can review documents, sign legally binding contracts, and monitor your portfolio entirely online from anywhere in the world.
