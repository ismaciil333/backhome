Questions

Everything you're wondering.

When does BackHome launch?

First homes go live in Mogadishu and Hargeisa in summer 2026, followed by Garowe shortly after. Everyone on the waitlist gets seven days of exclusive early access before the public launch.

How are properties verified?

Our team visits every home in person, takes photos in natural light, measures every room, and verifies the owner's ID and title documents. No listing goes live without this visit.

Can I pay in USD, EUR or GBP?

Yes — you can pay in your local currency via our protected payment system. Funds are held securely and only released to the owner at check-in.

What if something goes wrong when I arrive?

Our local team is on-call around the clock. If your property doesn't match what was listed, we'll relocate you within 24 hours or issue a full refund — no questions asked.

I own a property. How do I list?

Head to our property listing page. Submit your details and you'll hear from us within 48 hours to schedule an in-person verification visit.
